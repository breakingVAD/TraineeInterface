Breaking VAD is an engineering design team at Rice University. Their project, as part of the 2016-2017 Capstone Design course, was to develop tools for a simulation-based training to help medical professionals respond to complications arising in LVAD patients. The Simulated Pediatric LVAD Training System was developed in partnership with Texas Children's Hospital.
